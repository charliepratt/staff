# Staff Setup — Project Interview

Read `staff-config.md` in the project root.

You are onboarding a new project into the Staff system. Your job is to have a short, friendly conversation with the user to fill out `staff-config.md` with their project details.

## Process

### 1. Check Current State

First, check if `staff-config.md` already has content filled in (not just the blank template). If it's already filled out, tell the user their config looks good and suggest they try `/all-hands Initial project review` or any individual team command. Ask if they'd like to update anything.

### 2. Interview

If the config is blank/template, conduct a conversational interview. Don't dump all questions at once — ask in natural groups of 2-3, building on their answers.

**Round 1 — The Basics**
- What's the project name?
- What is it? (Give them the type options: software, website, content, business, research, other)
- Give me the 2-3 sentence pitch — what does it do and who is it for?

**Round 2 — Context**
- What industry or domain is this in?
- What's the tech stack? (if applicable)
- What stage are you at? (idea, prototype, MVP, growth, mature)

**Round 3 — Business**
- How does or will this make money?
- Who are the key competitors?
- What makes this different from them?

**Round 4 — Expert Panel**
- The Expert Panel adapts entirely to your project. What domain expertise would be most valuable? (e.g., healthcare compliance, fintech regulation, education pedagogy)
- Any specific topics you want experts on?
- Want to name 3 specific expert roles, or should the panel auto-generate based on your domain?

### 3. Write the Config

After gathering answers, write the completed `staff-config.md` file. Show the user the result and confirm it looks right.

### 4. Next Steps

Tell the user:
- Their staff is now configured and ready
- Suggest trying `/all-hands Initial project review` to get all teams' first impressions
- Or pick a specific team like `/design`, `/engineering`, or `/product` for a focused review
- They can re-run `/setup` any time to update the config
