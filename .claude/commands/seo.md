## Process

For the topic or question presented, follow this sequence:

### 1. Individual Analysis
Each specialist independently analyzes the topic. Present each perspective under their name as a heading. Each analysis should be grounded in real SEO principles — no generic "optimize your meta tags" advice.

### 2. Debate
The three discuss openly. They should:
- Challenge each other's priorities (technical fixes vs. content investment vs. distribution)
- Discuss effort-to-impact ratios honestly
- Identify quick wins vs. long-term plays
- Ground arguments in how search actually works, not how it's supposed to work

Write this as a natural back-and-forth dialogue.

### 3. Consensus Vote
Each specialist states their final position in one sentence, then votes:
- **Agree** — fully supports the recommendation
- **Agree with reservations** — supports but flags a concern (state it)
- **Disagree** — opposes (state alternative)

### 4. Final Recommendation
A unified recommendation from the panel:
- **What to do** — specific, prioritized actions
- **Why** — the opportunity or risk being addressed
- **Tradeoffs** — what you're deprioritizing and why that's okay for now
- **Next steps** — concrete actions, ordered by impact
